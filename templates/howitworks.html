How it Works?

A stepwise guide to our project

Step : 1
Fill in your Details

Fill in your details (Name and MIS) through the form provided in the 'Add new user' webpage. Once the 'Add user' button is clicked, the webcam will open and capture 30 images for the user, so that the face recognition model can be trained.

Step : 2
Data entry into the Database

The data (Name and MIS) is keyed in to our MySQL database and the images of the user captured through the webcam are saved into a local directory of the operating device.

Step : 3
Mark Attendance using Face recognition

Go to the Attendance webpage and click on 'Attendance' button. The webcam opens and captures frames from the live video. These frames are then used to extract faces by identifying the face points. These faces are then recognised by our face recognition model to mark the attendance.

Step : 4
Attendance Verification using Geolocation of the user.

The user then scans the QR code displayed on the screen to fill a google form (while keeping his mobile phone's location on). This location in the form of latitudes and longitudes is then compared against the latitudes and longitudes of the specific building in which the lecture is taking place.

Step : 5
Recording Attendance into the Database

If the location matches correctly and the face for the same user is identified, then the Attendance count in the MySQL table is incremented for the respective user. Also, the total lectures count is incremented in the Table.

Step : 6
Calculation and display of Attendance percentage.

The Attendance percentage for all users is calculated by the formula: (Lectures attended by user/ Total count of lectures)*100 . This Attendance percentage can be checked by the user by entering his/her MIS in the check attendance webpage. This data manipulation is carried out with the help of Javascript.
